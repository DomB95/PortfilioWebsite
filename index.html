About Me!

Hi, I'm Dominique Burns. a passionate and versatile Software Developer with a strong foundation in full-stack development, mobile apps, and game design. My journey began when I helped a friend build a website, and that spark quickly grew into a full-fledged passion for coding. Since then, I've developed skills in languages like C++, C#, Java, Kotlin, and Python, and gained hands-on experience with frameworks and tools such as Flutter, Firebase, Unity, and .NET. I'm currently earning my degree in Web Development from Full Sail University, where I've built projects ranging from portfolio sites to strategy games and mobile applications.
